About Us

Our organization aims to bring people working in digital media platforms under one umbrella.
We aim to unite this sector,

thus providing the necessary rights for workers as they operate for their respective employers.

From giving legal support to adequate advice, our organization relentlessly work for people working in the digital media sector. Often goes unnoticed, these people working in digital media are also facing several issues related to their jobs and style of operation. We are trying to empower them so that they can give their best to society. Our organization's operations are not confined to people working on digital media platforms. Instead, we cater to the overall interest of the general public.
